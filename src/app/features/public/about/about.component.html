About RajMango

Bangladesh's Finest Seasonal Mangoes, Delivered to Your Door

Who We Are

RajMango is a digital marketplace connecting mango enthusiasts with Bangladesh's finest seasonal mangoes. We were built to bring transparency, freshness, and convenience to how people discover, order, and receive premium mangoes — eliminating the guesswork and fragmented experience of traditional purchasing.

Our platform digitises the entire process: browse available varieties, place orders, track deliveries, and manage payments — all in one place.

Our Story

Why RajMango Exists

Every year, Bangladesh's mango season arrives with an abundance of extraordinary fruit varieties — Himsagar, Langra, Amrapali, Fazli, and more. Yet for most buyers, the experience of sourcing quality mangoes was fragmented: phone calls, Excel sheets, manual bank transfers, and no reliable way to confirm delivery.

RajMango was created to solve that problem. We built a platform that brings suppliers, customers, and courier partners together under one roof — making seasonal mango ordering as seamless as any modern e-commerce experience.

We operate as a marketplace — mangoes are sourced from certified agricultural suppliers and undergo quality inspections prior to shipment. RajMango does not produce the mangoes; we ensure they reach you in the best condition possible.

Quality & Freshness

Our Commitment to Quality

Every mango available on RajMango comes from verified agricultural suppliers operating in Bangladesh's premier growing regions. Quality is not an afterthought — it is built into every step of our supply chain:

  • Source Verification: All suppliers are assessed and certified before listing on the platform.
  • Quality Inspection: Produce undergoes grading and inspection before being accepted for fulfilment.
  • Careful Handling: Mangoes are packed and handled to maintain freshness throughout transit.
  • Issue Resolution: Any quality complaint reported within 24 hours of delivery is investigated and actioned promptly.

Seasonal agricultural produce varies naturally. We are transparent about that. When availability changes, we communicate immediately — no surprises.

Delivery

Getting Mangoes to You

RajMango partners with trusted courier providers across Bangladesh to ensure reliable and timely delivery. Our delivery process is built around transparency:

  • Orders are dispatched after payment is confirmed and quality checked.
  • Delivery timelines depend on your location and the courier's schedule.
  • You receive order status updates at each stage.
  • Delivery is subject to your providing an accurate and accessible address.

We work continuously to expand our courier network and coverage areas. If you are outside our current delivery zones, please contact us — we will do our best to assist.

Our Promise

Customer-First, Always

Every decision at RajMango is made with the customer in mind. That means:

  • Transparent pricing — the price you see is the price you pay. No hidden fees.
  • Honest availability — we only list what we can fulfil. If something changes, we tell you first.
  • Responsive support — a real team responding to your concerns, not an automated queue.
  • Fair refund and cancellation policies — because trust is earned, not assumed.

RajMango is a seasonal business. We operate with the mango harvest — and we take that responsibility seriously. Every order represents a customer who chose to trust us.
