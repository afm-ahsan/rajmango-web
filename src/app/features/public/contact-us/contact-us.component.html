Support

What We Can Help With

Our support team handles all customer-facing matters, including:

  • Order queries — status updates, changes before dispatch, or order confirmation issues
  • Delivery issues — delays, incorrect address, or courier-related problems
  • Payment and billing — payment verification, receipts, or refund status
  • Product quality — report a quality issue with your mango delivery
  • Account help — login problems, profile updates, or account access
  • General enquiries — anything else you'd like to ask

For quality complaints, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery and include photos of the affected produce. This helps us resolve your case quickly and improve our supply chain.
