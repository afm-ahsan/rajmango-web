Privacy Policy

How RajMango collects, uses, and protects your information

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 Last Updated: May 2026

Your Privacy Matters

RajMango is committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about what we collect and why. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our platform, place orders, or contact our support team.

By using RajMango, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, please contact us directly.

1. Introduction

Who We Are

RajMango is a digital marketplace for seasonal mangoes, operated in Bangladesh. We are responsible for the personal data you provide when creating an account, placing orders, or communicating with our team.

This policy applies to all personal data collected through our website and platform. It does not cover data practices of third-party services you may access via links on our platform.

2. Data We Collect

What Information We Collect

We collect personal data that you provide to us directly and data generated by your use of the platform:

Account Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Delivery address(es)
  • Account password (stored securely, never in plain text)

Order & Transaction Data

  • Order history and details
  • Payment method type (card, mobile banking, bank transfer)
  • Payment status and transaction references
  • Delivery address and courier selections

Usage Data

  • Pages and features accessed on the platform
  • Browser type and device information
  • IP address (for security purposes)
  • Session timestamps

We do not store full payment card numbers or sensitive financial credentials on our servers. Payment processing is handled through secure, encrypted third-party channels.

3. How We Use Your Information

Purpose of Data Processing

We use your personal data strictly for the following purposes:

  • Order fulfilment — processing, packing, and delivering your mango orders
  • Account management — maintaining your profile and preferences
  • Order communications — confirmation emails, delivery updates, and support replies
  • Payment processing — verifying and recording payment transactions
  • Customer support — resolving complaints, quality issues, and refund requests
  • Platform improvement — understanding how the platform is used to improve features
  • Legal compliance — meeting applicable regulatory and tax obligations in Bangladesh

We do not use your personal data for unsolicited marketing or sell it to third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Storage & Security

How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Access controls: Personal data is accessible only to authorised RajMango staff with a legitimate need.
  • Secure servers: Data is stored on secured servers with firewall protection and monitoring.
  • Password hashing: Your account password is stored as an irreversible hash — we cannot read it.
  • No data sales: Your data is never sold, rented, or traded to third parties for commercial gain.

Despite our best efforts, no system is completely secure. If you suspect unauthorised access to your account, please contact us immediately at contact@ajwadtech.com.

5. Your Rights

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data held by RajMango:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your account.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from any marketing communications at any time.
  • Objection: Object to specific processing activities where you have grounds to do so.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@ajwadtech.com with your registered email address and the nature of your request. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

How We Use Cookies

RajMango uses cookies strictly for platform functionality:

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in during your browsing session.
  • Authentication tokens: Securely maintain your login state between visits.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your platform preferences (e.g., language, display settings).

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies that profile your behaviour across other websites.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality, including your ability to log in.

7. Third-Party Services

Who We Share Data With

RajMango shares limited personal data with third parties only where necessary to fulfil your order or comply with legal requirements:

  • Courier providers: Your name, phone number, and delivery address are shared with our courier partners to arrange delivery.
  • Payment processors: Transaction references and order amounts are passed to payment processors. We do not share your full card or banking credentials.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose data to comply with a court order, legal obligation, or government request.

We do not share personal data beyond what is strictly necessary for the purposes above. All third-party partners are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable data protection standards.

8. Changes to This Policy

Policy Updates

RajMango may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Notify registered customers via email if changes materially affect how we use your data.

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically. Continued use of the platform after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
